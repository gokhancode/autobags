# ⚙️ Basic Mode

Basic mode is designed for users who want automated trading without tweaking settings. Pick your risk appetite and the bot handles the rest.

***

## Risk levels

### 🟢 Low
Best for: conservative users, smaller accounts, first-timers

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Stop loss | -5% |
| Take profit | +15% |
| Min intel score | 75/100 |
| Max balance deployed | 50% |

The bot is more selective — fewer trades, safer picks.

***

### 🟡 Medium (default)
Best for: balanced risk/reward

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Stop loss | -8% |
| Take profit | +25% |
| Min intel score | 65/100 |
| Max balance deployed | 80% |

The recommended starting point for most users.

***

### 🔴 High
Best for: experienced traders, higher risk tolerance, degen mode

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Stop loss | -12% |
| Take profit | +40% |
| Min intel score | 55/100 |
| Max balance deployed | 95% |

Higher potential upside, but larger drawdowns possible.

***

## Switching modes

You can switch between Basic and Advanced at any time from your dashboard settings. Your risk level is saved immediately — the bot uses the latest settings on its next tick.

Want more control? Switch to [Advanced Mode](advanced-mode.md) to customize every parameter independently.
{% endhint %}
