# 💳 Funding Your Wallet

## Your deposit address

When you sign up, AUTOBAGS generates a dedicated Solana wallet for your account. Your **deposit address** is shown on your dashboard — it's a standard Solana address (base58, starts with a letter or number).

Send SOL to this address from any source:
- Exchanges (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, Bybit)
- Wallets (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack)
- Other AUTOBAGS accounts

***

## How much to deposit?

| Amount | Use case |
|---|---|
| 0.05 SOL | Testing — covers gas but limits trade size |
| 0.1–0.5 SOL | Good starting range for Basic mode |
| 0.5–5 SOL | Comfortable range for full Advanced mode |

{% hint style="info" %}
The bot always keeps a minimum reserve of **0.05 SOL** for transaction fees. This is never traded.
{% endhint %}

***

## When does my balance show up?

Solana transactions confirm in ~1 second. Your dashboard balance refreshes every time you load the page. If you don't see it within 30 seconds, check the transaction on [Solscan](https://solscan.io).

***

## Withdrawing funds

To withdraw, export your private key (requires 2FA), import it into Phantom or Solflare, and send funds wherever you like. Full self-custody at any time.

***

## Gas fees

Each Solana transaction costs approximately **0.000005 SOL** (~$0.0004). These are deducted automatically from your balance.

AUTOBAGS charges a **1.5% platform fee** per trade, collected via Bags.fm fee sharing. See [Fees](../platform/fees.md) for details.
