# 🚀 Quick Start

Get from zero to automated trading in under 5 minutes.

***

## Step 1 — Create an account

Go to [autobags.io](https://autobags.io) and click **"Start trading free"**.

Enter:
- A username (used to log in)
- A password (8+ characters)
- Email (optional)

{% hint style="info" %}
You don't need an existing crypto wallet. AUTOBAGS generates one for you automatically.
{% endhint %}

***

## Step 2 — Set up 2FA

After signing up, you'll see a **QR code**. Scan it with:
- [Google Authenticator](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.authenticator2)
- [Authy](https://authy.com/)
- Any TOTP-compatible app

Enter the 6-digit code shown in your app to confirm. This activates your account.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Save your 2FA setup.** If you lose access to your authenticator app without a backup, you won't be able to log in or export your wallet.
{% endhint %}

***

## Step 3 — Fund your wallet

After confirming 2FA, you'll see your **deposit address** — a Solana wallet created just for you.

Send SOL to this address from any exchange or wallet (Coinbase, Binance, Phantom, etc.).

- **Minimum recommended:** 0.05 SOL (covers gas + first trade)
- **Recommended start:** 0.1–1 SOL

Your balance appears on the dashboard within ~30 seconds of confirmation.

***

## Step 4 — Configure & activate

Go to **Bot settings** on your dashboard:

- **Basic mode:** pick Low / Medium / High risk and you're done
- **Advanced mode:** fine-tune stop loss, take profit, intel score threshold, and more

Make sure **"Bot active"** is toggled on.

***

## Step 5 — Watch it work

The agent runs every minute. Check your dashboard for:
- Live SOL balance
- Trade history
- P&L

That's it. The bot handles everything else.
